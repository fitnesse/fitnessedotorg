To diplay todays date:

Markup Text
Displayed as
Today is !today. Today is 01 Mar, 2025.
XML is !today -xml. XML is 2025-03-01T16:17:31.
Right now it is !today -t. Right now it is 01 Mar, 2025 16:17.
Tomorrow is !today +1. Tomorrow is 02 Mar, 2025.
Last week was !today -7. Last week was 22 Feb, 2025.
The month is !today (MMM). The month is Mar.

As you can see, you can add or subtract days. You can include the time with -t. And you can use your own format for the time. (See SimpleDataFormat for the syntax.)
